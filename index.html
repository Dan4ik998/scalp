How does it work?

We use specially crafted sterile needles and organic pigments that match your natural hair color, which is then inserted into the scalp (epidermal level) safely and efficiently to give the impression of full and natural hair growth.

The Scalp micropigmentation process is a relatively pain-free procedure generally applied over 3 sessions depending on the extent of hair loss that needs to be treated. Each session takes an average of 2-5 hours and 7-12 days is needed between each session for the scalp to properly heal and blend in naturally.

Restore your hairline

The hairline is of great importance to your appearance and our highly skilled technicans at Micro Skalp can re-create a fully customized hairline according to your wishes.

There are many options when it comes to choosing your hairline. While some of our clients want a natural and soft hairline, others might want a more youthful, sharp and defined hairline. All hairlines are unique and costumized for each client.
